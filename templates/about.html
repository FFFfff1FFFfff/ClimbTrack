About ClimbTrack

Empowering climbers to track their progress, connect with the community, and reach new heights together.

Welcome to Our Climbing Community

ClimbTrack was born from a passion for climbing and a desire to help fellow climbers achieve their goals. Our community brings together climbers of all levels, from beginners taking their first steps on the wall to seasoned athletes pushing their limits on challenging routes.

We believe that climbing is more than just a sport – it's a community, a lifestyle, and a journey of personal growth. Whether you're into bouldering, sport climbing, or traditional routes, ClimbTrack provides the tools and community support you need to excel.

Founded by climbers, for climbers, we understand the unique challenges and triumphs that come with this incredible sport. Our platform is designed to celebrate every achievement, no matter how small, and to help you track your progress as you grow stronger and more confident on the wall.

Our Vision & Features

📊

Progress Tracking

Log your climbing sessions, track grades, and monitor your improvement over time. See your personal bests and celebrate every milestone on your climbing journey.

📱

Session Logging

Easily record your climbs with photos, grades, notes, and climbing type. Keep detailed records of your training sessions and achievements.

🏆

Community Rankings

Compete with fellow climbers, see community rankings, and get inspired by others' achievements in both bouldering and sport climbing.

📝

Movement Notes

Document beta sequences, technique tips, and movement insights. Build your personal climbing knowledge base for future reference.

👥

Crowd Detection

Check gym capacity and activity levels to plan your sessions when the gym is less crowded for optimal training conditions.

📈

Statistics & Insights

Get detailed analytics on your climbing performance, session frequency, and progress trends to optimize your training regimen.
